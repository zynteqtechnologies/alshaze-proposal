03 — Key Feature

Multi-Vessel Allocation

Scenario A — Single customer order split across two vessels
Customer orders 200 MT of HDPE. 120 MT arriving on Vessel A, 80 MT on Vessel B. One PFI is created for 200 MT. At deal confirmation, the system allocates 120 MT from Vessel A and 80 MT from Vessel B (pre-allocated with ETA). Both allocations tracked under the same deal. Dispatch in two shipments, both linked to same PFI — customer sees one order, you see two shipments.
Scenario B — Multiple customer orders from a single vessel
One vessel arrives with 300 MT of PP. Three customers have orders (100, 120, 80 MT). Separate PFIs created for each customer. Stock arrival logged (vessel name, B/L, total quantity). System allocates quantity from that vessel's stock to each deal. All deals show same vessel name and B/L as source — full traceability.
04 — Platform

Core Modules

📦
Inventory & Stock
Grade-wise management (HDPE, LDPE, LLDPE, PP, PET). Dual source: warehouse / vessel with ETA. Real-time total / allocated / free / dispatched. Tranche management, low-stock alerts, full audit trail.
🔄
Deal & CRM Pipeline
PFI creation with custom payment terms per deal. 9-stage pipeline with allocation engine (prevents double-booking). Customer profiles, full deal timeline, and RCN auto-filing.
🚛
Dispatch & Truck Management
30-ton MOQ, dedicated truck per deal. Driver + truck number + destination. One driver can be assigned to multiple deals — single login, one update form, applied to all linked deals.
📄
RCN & Document Auto-filing
Staff uploads RCN and SAD. System auto-creates an ImageKit folder named with RCN number + deal ID. All docs (PFI, SAD, RCN, weighbridge, POD, invoices) stored inside — no manual folder creation.
📍
One-Tap GPS Driver Tracking
Driver opens unique link, taps "Send My Location". HTML5 Geolocation API captures GPS coordinates + timestamp. Staff clicks "View on Map" → OpenStreetMap shows exact position. Free, no API key required.
📱
Driver Portal
Unique mobile link per driver. Shows customer, destination, product, quantity. Two buttons: "Send My Location" and "Upload POD". Auto-linked to all relevant deals. No training required.
👤
Customer Portal (Read-only)
Customer logs in, sees only their own orders. Real-time status: Confirmed → Loaded → On the Way → Delivered. Downloads PFI, POD, invoice anytime. Sees current shipment location.
💰
Payment & Installments
Advance or credit modes. Custom terms per deal (30/60/75 days RCN, advance + balance). Installment schedule log with amount, date, reference. Overdue alerts + outstanding report.
📊
Analytics & BI
Daily P&L dashboard. Revenue by country and by grade. Outstanding collections overview. Deal conversion rate tracking. Stock movement chart.
✉️
Email Automation
Auto email on RCN upload: "Payment timer started – due [date]" with RCN PDF. Auto email on POD upload. Auto payment reminders before due date and overdue alerts. Full message log per deal.
